Setup Show whitespaces It would be clever to check the option “Options->Code->Display->Show whitespace characters” in RStudio. Otherwise you will never know how many whitespaces you put at the end of a line, and then you may spend a lot of time figuring errors in paragraphs formatting. The rule of two spaces to end a paragraph is not the best feature of the markup language.
